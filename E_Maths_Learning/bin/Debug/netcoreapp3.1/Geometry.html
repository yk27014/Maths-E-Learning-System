Equation of a Straight Standard straight line equation.
This form of the straight line equation is often used to derive the standard straight line equation.
Variables
  • m = gradient of the line
  • c = the value of y when x is 0 (y intercept)
  • y1 = the y value of a point on the line.
  • x1 = the x value of a point on the line.
